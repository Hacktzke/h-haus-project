Our Story

H.Haus is a small family run business established in 2020 that designs, imports and sells hand crafted interior items within New Zealand. The H in the name stands for the director's surname being Hertzke, and the Haus means house in German, which is the director's family heritage. The business started from a personal passion for interior design with Chanel redesigning her personal homes, making her decide to create her dream business of selling beautiful interior styling items. The business is based in Auckland but offers shipping throughout New Zealand. H.Haus sells only the highest quality hand crafted products from pots and vessels to chairs and furniture.
